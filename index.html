Why CBT?

Why most ADHD therapy doesn't work — and what does

Standard talk therapy wasn't built for the ADHD brain. CBT-ADHD was. Developed at Massachusetts General Hospital and Harvard Medical School by Dr. Steven Safren, it's the only behavioral treatment for adult ADHD validated in a large, randomized controlled trial published in JAMA.

  • →
    Thought restructuring — Identify and reframe unhelpful beliefs about your productivity, worth, and capability.
  • →
    Behavioral activation — Build momentum through structured action, not motivation.
  • →
    Skill generalization — Learn strategies you can actually apply across every area of your life.
  • →
    ADHD-specific adaptations — Traditional CBT adapted for shorter attention spans, variable motivation, and rejection sensitivity.
78% of patients report significant improvement in daily functioning after CBT

Our Approach

Built on Research from MGH & Harvard Medical School

Silver Health delivers CBT-ADHD — the manualized treatment protocol developed by Dr. Steven Safren and colleagues at Massachusetts General Hospital's Cognitive Behavioral Therapy Program and Harvard Medical School. Dr. Safren's research established CBT as the gold standard in behavioral treatment for adult ADHD.

In his landmark 2010 randomized controlled trial published in JAMA, Dr. Safren demonstrated that CBT-ADHD produced significant improvement in 67% of medication-treated adults — more than twice the rate of the comparison group — with gains sustained at 12-month follow-up. Our clinicians are trained directly in this evidence base, not a generic approximation of it.
